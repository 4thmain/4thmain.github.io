Talking to reporters after launching the project, the DGP said it is nationwide project connected to MHA,NCRB, New Delhi and all State capitals and Union Territories of the country and is a significant landmark in making police more accessible transparent and citizen friendly. According to West Godavari district Superintendent of Police M. Ramesh, the CCTNS project came into existence with three initial featuresâ€”Andhra Pradesh Police Messaging System (APPMS), an investigator tool to track the identity of mobile phone and vehicle users and the Andhra Pradesh Police Website.
